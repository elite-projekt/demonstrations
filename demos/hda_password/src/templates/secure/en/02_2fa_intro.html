2FA Introduction

We will use the app Google Authenticator in this example.

The Google Authenticator is available for both Android and iOS.

Google Play Store

App Store

There are other free 2FA apps. If you want to use another app, find out about the app's security beforehand. A good basic requirement is if the app has an open source licence. You can find more 2FA apps by searching for authentication in the Play Store or the Apple App Store.

  1. Open your Google Authenticator app on your smartphone.
  2. When you first start up, you will see a welcome screen. Click on Start now.
  3. Now click on scan QR-Code.
  4. The app asks for access to your camera. Confirm this permission. Now the app opens your camera..
  5. Now scan the QR code displayed on the monitor.
  6. A new account is created and you see a 6-digit number - the Security Token
    Each token is valid for 60 seconds.
  7. Enter the code in the input field and confirm the entry.
